Chairman's Message :

As the new academic year kick in on an outstanding note, KCC feels elated to welcome all its new and returning students, faculty, staff and parents. With the help and pro-activeness of you, the students and everyone else, we hope to accomplish the mission of holistic education approaches, constant evolution of minds and growth towards future.
